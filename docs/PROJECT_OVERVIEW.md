# Project Overview

## Project Name

AI 销售知识库 + AI 客户匹配判断系统

English name:

AI Sales Knowledge Base + AI Customer Matching Judgment System

## Product Positioning

This project is an AI-powered B2B sales assistant.

It helps users organize company and product information into a structured sales
knowledge base, then uses that knowledge to find, analyze, score, and explain
potential B2B leads.

The system is not a full CRM and not a fully automated email platform.

The first version focuses on helping users make better sales development decisions before outreach.

## Target Users

The initial target users are B2B companies that want to find new customers more efficiently.

The first market entry can focus on Chinese companies selling to overseas
markets, but the system itself should be designed as a global B2B sales
assistant.

Target users may include:

- Startup founders
- Small B2B sales teams
- Export sales teams
- Business development teams
- B2B SaaS companies
- Manufacturing or service companies looking for overseas customers

## Core Problem

B2B sales teams often have several problems:

1. Company and product information is scattered.
2. Salespeople do not always know which customers are a good fit.
3. Lead lists often contain many low-quality companies.
4. Manual website research is slow.
5. Sales emails are generic and not based on real customer evidence.
6. It is difficult to explain why one company is worth contacting.

## Core Value

This system helps users:

1. Convert company information into a structured sales knowledge base.
2. Create clear product cards.
3. Define sales campaigns.
4. Discover potential customer companies.
5. Analyze customer websites.
6. Score customer fit using AI.
7. Show matching reasons and risk notes.
8. Generate personalized Gmail drafts for approved leads.

## MVP Product Direction

The MVP should complete one clear workflow:

Company information input → AI knowledge draft → user confirmation → product
card → campaign → lead discovery → customer analysis → AI scoring → user
review → Gmail draft.

The MVP should be useful even as a single-user prototype.

## Non-goals

The MVP is not:

- A full CRM
- A full email marketing system
- A multi-agent automation platform
- A LinkedIn automation tool
- A Google Sheets workflow
- A complete account-based sales platform
- A replacement for human sales judgment

## Important Principles

1. AI assists the user but does not replace user review.
2. AI results must include reasons and evidence.
3. Outreach emails must be generated as drafts only.
4. The system must be modular and easy to change.
5. The MVP must stay focused and avoid unnecessary features.
6. The system should be designed for future production deployment from the beginning.
